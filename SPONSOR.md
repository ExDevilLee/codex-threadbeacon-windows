# 支持 ThreadBeacon

ThreadBeacon 是一个免费、开源的本地 Windows 工具。所有功能都不会因为是否赞助而改变，
项目也不会读取赞助身份或提供付费解锁。

## 现在可以怎样支持

- 在 [GitHub](https://github.com/ExDevilLee/codex-threadbeacon-windows) 为项目点 Star。
- 把项目分享给需要同时观察多个 Codex 任务状态的用户。
- 使用 Issue 提交不包含任务内容、本机路径或凭据的 Bug 和功能建议。
- 通过 Pull Request 改进代码、测试、文档或翻译。

## 付费赞助

付费赞助渠道暂未启用。以后如果增加 GitHub Sponsors、Buy Me a Coffee、微信或支付宝等
渠道，会继续遵守以下原则：

- 完全自愿，不解锁功能，不影响免费使用。
- 不提供与数字内容或服务绑定的回报。
- 不在 App 主窗口展示付款广告或二维码。

## Support ThreadBeacon

ThreadBeacon is a free, open-source local Windows utility. Every feature remains available whether
or not you sponsor the project, and the app does not identify sponsors or unlock paid features.

### Ways to Help Today

- Star the project on [GitHub](https://github.com/ExDevilLee/codex-threadbeacon-windows).
- Share it with people who monitor multiple Codex tasks at once.
- Use Issues for privacy-safe bug reports and feature requests.
- Improve code, tests, documentation, or translations through a pull request.

### Financial Sponsorship

Financial sponsorship is not enabled yet. If GitHub Sponsors, Buy Me a Coffee, WeChat Pay, or
Alipay is added later, support will remain entirely optional, unlock no features, and provide no
reward tied to digital content or services. Payment advertising and QR codes will stay out of the
main app window.
